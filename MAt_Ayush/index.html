Maggi

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles and seasoning to the boiling water.
  3. Cook for about 2 minutes or until the noodles are soft.
  4. Stir well and serve hot.

Coffee

  1. Boil water and add it to your filter with coffee grounds.
  2. Allow the coffee to brew for about 4-5 minutes.
  3. Pour the brewed coffee into a cup and add sugar and milk as per taste.
  4. Stir well and serve hot.

Omelette

  1. Beat 2 eggs with salt, pepper, and a tablespoon of milk.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and pour the egg mixture into it.
  3. Cook over medium heat until the edges start to lift from the pan.
  4. Flip the omelette and cook for another minute.
  5. Serve hot with a side of toasted bread.
